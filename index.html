Meet the Team

Experienced dentists. Warm, personal care.

Our doctors combine advanced training with a calm, patient-first approach so every visit feels comfortable and clear.

Dr. Nina Aks, DMD

General, cosmetic, implant, and restorative dentistry

Dr. Nina Aks earned her degree in Dental Medicine from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine. Before coming to the United States, she graduated from the Medical University of St. Petersburg in Russia with double doctorate degrees in Medicine and Dentistry.

She has over 20 years of experience and completed advanced post-graduate education with the Dawson Academy, where she earned Scholar status, along with additional programs with the Spear Institute, Massad Learning Center, and Dr. Dick Barnes Group.

Dr. Aks continues advanced dental studies every year so patients benefit from the latest techniques in cosmetic dentistry, implants, and prosthodontics.

Dr. Steven Liu, DMD

Implants, periodontics, prosthetics, and fixed prosthodontics

Dr. Steven Liu earned his degree in Dental Medicine from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine and completed post-doctoral certificates in Periodontics, Prosthetics, Fixed Prosthodontics, and implant surgical and prosthetic training.

He served as an assistant professor and clinical educator in Restorative Dentistry at the University of Pennsylvania and directed the Implant Dentistry Course. He now serves as course director for Restorative and Esthetic Concern for Provisional Fabrication and Esthetic Implant Dentistry at Penn.

Dr. Liu received the Joseph L. T. Appleton Award for excellence in clinical instruction in 2001, 2002, and 2004, and was named an American Top Dentist in 2005, 2006, and 2007.

Dr. Ghulam Murtaza, DMD

Endodontics and microsurgical root canal care

Dr. Ghulam Murtaza earned his degree in Dental Medicine with honors from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine and completed his specialty residency in endodontics at the University of Maryland Dental School.

During dental school, he received an award for excellence in endodontics and became a member of the Omega Kappa Upsilon dental honorary society. He has extensive experience performing endodontic microsurgical procedures.

Dr. Murtaza stays current with the latest advances in endodontics through continuing education and local and national meetings. Outside the office, he enjoys soccer and time outdoors with his family.
